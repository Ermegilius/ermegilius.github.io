The goal of the project is to ensure the protection of the rights and welfare of Russian and Russian-speaking peoples, foreign peoples in the Middle East, as well as to expand understanding of the linguistic and cultural needs of the Russian and Russian-speaking minorities. In addition, the organization strives to ensure the peaceful resolution of international agreements through circulation. To achieve its goals, the Company can:
- Organize awareness-raising, training and consulting;.
activities on democracy, social studies and multicultural coexistence.
- Organize meetings with government officials and responsible representatives of countries around the world and other countries in order to consider and solve problems and tasks facing both government bodies and the Russian-speaking minority as a whole.
- Conduct seminars and practices, promote intercultural dialogue and mutual understanding and aimed at discussing issues, modern rights, promotion of culture and the Russian-speaking minority.
- Participate in public discussions and provide assistance in the preparation of electronic payments, the current rights of the Russian-speaking minority.
- Advocate for the preservation of dual citizenship and protect civil rights such as the right to freedom of movement and maintenance of family ties.
- Carry out regular assemblies, organize fairs and other events to attract money.
- Accept financial assistance, donations and inheritances.
- Organize business trips for its members aimed at fulfilling the tasks facing the Society.
- Volunteer work.
- Conclude sponsorship agreements.
- Conduct paid events.
- Distribute informational materials and printed materials.
- Cooperate with associations and ensure similar character both in Finland and abroad.
