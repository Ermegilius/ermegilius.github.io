Fundamental human rights and freedoms must be recognized and protected regardless of citizenship, language, religion, nationality or any other aspect.
A person who shares the statutory goals of the Organization and undertakes to follow the charter of the Organization can become a full member of the Organization. An individual or legal entity who is ready to support the goals of the Organization and take part in its activities can become an associated member of the Organization. The General Meeting may nominate a person who has made a special contribution to the activities of the Organization to the position of honorary chairman, as well as honorary member. The organization has the right to terminate the membership of a person whose words or actions damage its reputation. The decision regarding the fact and severity of damage caused is made by the Board.
